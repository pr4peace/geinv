System Architecture

How the pieces connect — from document upload to email reminder

INPUT
📄 Agreement Upload
PDF or scanned image of signed investment agreement
→
AI EXTRACTION
🤖 Claude AI
Reads agreement, extracts all key fields: parties, amount, rate, dates, payout schedule, TDS terms
→
REVIEW
✏️ Confirm & Edit
You review extracted fields, fix anything, then confirm — agreement saved to database
STORAGE
🗄️ Supabase
Database: All investment records, parties, terms
File Storage: Original agreement PDFs
Scheduler: Upcoming reminder queue
→
AUTO-SCHEDULE
📅 Reminder Engine
On save, auto-generates reminder dates for every critical date:
3 months → 1 month → 2 weeks → 1 week before
→
DELIVERY
📧 Resend (Email)
Sends reminder emails automatically at the right time. WhatsApp added in Phase 2.
WHAT YOU SEE
📊 Dashboard
All investments at a glance — active, matured, upcoming payouts, TDS summary
📋 Agreement Detail
Full terms, payout schedule, TDS calculations, original scanned document
🗓️ Calendar View
Monthly calendar showing all critical dates across all agreements — colour-coded by type
🔔 Reminder Log
History of all reminders sent, upcoming reminders queued, ability to send manually
